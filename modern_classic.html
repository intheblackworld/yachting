    Modern Classic Gran Torino
    Design inspired by the traditional sports car series in the 1970s. Rough but also elegant lines of the hull and the large area of wood veneer on the ship-side show a classic and retro image, which shows a highlights of the owner’s unique taste. Spacious sun deck, open windows and large areas of the salon allow people to fully enjoy the sunlight; the kitchen set which is located at the poop provides a smooth and comfortable circulation just like at home.
